SMITH & NEPHEW TOWER

Tower Setup

  1. Plug in Tower into the red wall outlet
  2. Make sure that the master switch (located on the side) is on
  3. Turn on the 660 Box
  4. Plug in the HDMI (or DVI) cable into the Boom Aux or Wall Aux
  5. Go to nurses Touchpanel and make sure to route the scope tower image to the monitors you need
  6. Once the 660 is booted up then you can move on to step 2 and input the patient information

660 Patient Input

  1. Press the "Patient" button
  2. Input:
    • Last Name
    • First Name
    • MRN
    • Procedure
    • Doctor
  3. Press "Save" then hit "Yes"

Printing Settings for Case

  1. Press "Print"
  2. On this page you can then change:
    • Number of images per page: All will use 6 except for Dr. Hackett who has 9
    • Turn Auto Print to "ON"
    • Number of copies is usually 1
  3. At the end of the case you can print the remaining images by pressing the "Print Remaining Images" button

Re-Printing Images

  1. Press the "Print" button
  2. Turn the Auto Print "OFF"
  3. Now press the "Print" button below to print all images from that case
  4. **Make sure the printer is on**

Patient Searching

  1. Press "Search" button
  2. You can choose which criteria to search with but I usually do by Date
  3. Select the patient you need
  4. Press the button you need: Print, Open, Save, etc.

Swapping 560 and Lens Box

  1. Open back door of the tower and look to see which of the boxes is currently connected
  2. There will be 3 types of cables that are plugged in:
    • DVI Cable: outputs the video of the box to the monitors in the room. (what you plug into the boom or wall aux)
    • SDI Cable: outputs the video to the 660 box to capture the proper images / videos. (where you put the patient information)
    • Accessory Cables: used to capture images and videos when buttons are pressed on the doctor's camera head
  3. Simply move each of these cables to same ports just in the other box. They should be color coded with tape to make moving easy and less confusing.
  4. Check the monitor to make sure you see the proper color bars for that box

Tower Printer Paper & Ink

  1. Open the bottom tray on the tower to see the Ink and Paper stash
  2. To change paper:
    • Pull out tray on the bottom of the printer and repalce with new paper
    • Make sure the paper is SHINY SIDE DOWN so that it prints on the correct side of paper
  3. To change Ink:
    • Open the lid on the printer and wait for the ink cartriges to center in the middle
    • If you see any inks that have blinking lights that means they are low or empty
    • Simply press the tab and pull the ink out and then replace with same color from bottom tray

    **Get more Ink and Paper as needed from the Cabinet above the Arthrex printer located in PACU**
