DR. LISLE

Below are videos and step-by-step directions on how to set up cases for Dr. Lisle and how to use / troubleshoot equipment that she commonly uses.

ROBOTIC

Stryker / Robot Setup

  1. Turn on the Stryker Boxes
    • They will use the stryker laparoscopic equipment first before the robot
  2. Go over to Nurse Touchpanel and route Stryker to all the monitors in the room
  3. Nurse will then change to the BOOM 4n1 once they switch to the robot
  4. Go over to the Aida and press the RECORD button. Dr. Lisle records all of his cases
  5. At the end of the case Dr. Lisle usually saves to his USB Drive

Aida Patient Input

  1. Go over to the Aida and input the Patient Information
  2. First Name, Last Name, MRN, Doctor
  3. Turn ON the Stryker boxes that are on the Boom
  4. Go over to the Nurse Touchpanel and route to ALL the screens in the room

PRINTING SUPPORT

Printing Images

  1. Go over to the Aida and press "Finish"
  2. This will automatically print the images for the case

Aida Re-Printing

  1. Select the file cabinet button in top right side of screen
  2. Look for the patient name that you need and select the box on the right left side
  3. Make sure to print with the "Standard" button layout selected

Troubleshooting Printer

MAKE VIDEO

  1. Make sure printer is ON by looking for light on the power button
  2. Add paper as needed. More paper is located above the computer by MOR Room 4
  3. Lift top flap and wait for the Ink to center and check to see if any are blinking which indicates that they are low / empty and need to be replaced.

Printer Problem Checklist

  1. Is Printer ON?
    • Make sure light is on and power cord is plugged into the back
  2. Is there paper in the printer?
    • Check the paper holder and get paper if needed
  3. Has the case been ended?
    • Make sure to press "Finish" at the end of the case to send to the printer
  4. Is there a red error light coming from the the button below the power button?
