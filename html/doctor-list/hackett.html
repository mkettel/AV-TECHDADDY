DR. HACKETT

Below are videos and step-by-step directions on how to set up cases for Dr. Hackett and how to use / troubleshoot equipment that he commonly uses.

SCOPE CASES

Smith and Nephew Tower Setup

  1. Plug in the tower to a RED outlet
  2. Make sure that master switch on the side of the tower is ON
  3. Press the power button on the front of the 660 Box to turn on
  4. While the 660 boots up, open the back door of the tower to make sure that the 560 Box is connected
    • Click here to see video on how to swap the LENS and 560 Boxes
  5. On the 660, press "New Patient" and input the patient information
    • First, Last Name
    • MRN
    • Doctor Name
    • Procedure Type
  6. Check the Printing settings by pressing the "print" button
    • Make sure that Auto Print is ON
    • Set number of images per page to 9 and copies to 1
  7. Take the HDMI or DVI Cable and plug it into the Boom or Wall aux to route to the desired monitors in the room

Re-Printing a Previous Case

  1. On the 660 Home screen press the "Search" button
  2. From here you an go in and search for patients by date or name
  3. Once you have searched and found the patient you're looking for, select the patient and you can choose to Open, Save, or Print so select "Print"

Setup Checklist

  1. Is the Patient Information plugged into the 660 Box?
  2. Is the 560 Box connected and NOT the Lens box?
  3. Is the video from the tower routed to desired monitors in the room?
  4. Are the printing settings correct?

    • 1 Copy
