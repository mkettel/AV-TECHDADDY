DR. GALLIZZI

Below are videos and step-by-step directions on how to set up cases for Dr. Gallizzi and how to use / troubleshoot equipment that he commonly uses.

MICROSCOPE CASES

MOR Microscope Setup

  1. Plug the microscope into a RED outlet
  2. Take the HDMI Cable and plug it into the Boom 2n1 panel
    • Make sure to do the loop around the pillar to avoid strain on the hdmi cable during case
  3. Turn ON the microscope from the switch down towards the bottom
  4. Go over to the Nurse Touchpanel and route to the monitors you need

ENDOSCOPIC CASES

Arthrex Tower Setup

  1. Plug in BOTH plugs into RED outlets
  2. Turn on the Arthrex Box and Monitor if not on
  3. Plug in the HDMI or DVI cable (if desired) to the BOOM or WALL Aux and route the scope video to monitors in the room
  4. Plug in the Patient information into the Arthrex tablet
    • First, Last Name
    • MRN
    • Doctor
    • Procedure
  5. Press "Start Case"
